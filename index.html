VISIT MÉRIDA...

The safest city in Mexico

Mérida, the capital of the state of Yucatan, was founded on some Mayan ruins (T'Hó) and since the colonial period has been one of the most important cities in México. In this city you can see a huge cultural heritage, which is a mixture of the Mayan and Spanish cultures. You can see this cultural legacy in its architecture, food and traditions

Best Foods

  • Cochinita Pibil: the most famous food in Yucatán, It's a seasoned meat that can be served as a taco or torta
  • Poc-Chuc: a delicious grilled pork with a wide variety of complements
  • Panuchos: fried tortilla with beans inside, various ingredients can be placed on top

Recommended estaurants:
